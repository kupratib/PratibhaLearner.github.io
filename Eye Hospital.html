Eye Hospital App

This is a 3d app, in which nurse will visit to the patient bed and know pateint's condition and the steps done by Doctor for eye treatment and also what are the next steps doctor going to do according to it she will prepare the ICU.

When nurse will reach to the patient's bed she will able to see the patient board, where copleted steps are given, she is also able to see the other 7 steps in form of button by clicking on which she will reach to that room with patient for next step treatment.

Below are the seven steps:

  1. Physician Clearance.
  2. Informed Consent Signed
  3. Patient Verification
  4. Dressed for Operation Room
  5. Surgical Checklist
  6. Based on the type of Anethesia, Preopeye cleaning need to done
  7. Intill betadine drops and Sent him/her to OR
